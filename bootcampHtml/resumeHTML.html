  • LC4 Cipher Decryption
    • Decrypting the cipher starts with a 6x6 grid representing a specific alphabet. Given an encrypted string the challenge is to find its decrypted partner
    • The program places a marker in the top left corner of the grid. Then it performs mathematical operations on the encrypted character to determine the vertical and horizontal movement from the marekr
    • Using that movement we are able to find the decrypted character
    • Afterwards we shift the column of the encrypted character and the row of the decrypted character
    • Then we move the marker and repeat the process until the string is decrypted
